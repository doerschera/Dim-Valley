This is Dim Valley.

You've managed to discover the words needed for the chant, awesome. But unfortunately, this ritual can't be performed in just any old space-- there's only one spot in town imbued with enough magic to the kind of complete darkness headed your way. Unfortunately, no one thought to clearly label this hallowed ground. You know that there are five streets you need to take to reach the spot, but not which streets these are.

Because Dim Valley is a perfectly planned suburban settlement, there are exactly four houses on the left-hand side of each street. Each house on the correct streets is one of six colors, though some or all of the houses might be the same color. Guess the correct order of the house colors to choose which street to take.

Watch out: The stars are safe, but a black cloud has begun to hover over the town. With each wrong turn you make, it gets closer and closer to town...and so does darkness. After five wrong guesses, the town is lost.
